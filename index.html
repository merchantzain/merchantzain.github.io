about me


Hey there!

As you can probably tell, my name is Zain. I'm an undergraduate Computer Science student at The University of Texas at Dallas. I also spend semesters working as a co-op at NASA Langley Research Center.

My primary interests are in embedded, autonomous, and high performance computing systems. Though I enjoy just about any challenging and worthwhile problem! Outside of NASA, my previous research experiences have been in using technology to help understand and better protect the world around us.

Other than Computer Science-y stuff, I enjoy travelling, amatuer astronomy, and making things.
